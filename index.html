Hi, I'm Steven Miller!

I'm a 28 year old aspiring Web Developer who also works as a barista at Starbucks Coffee Company in San Diego, California. Although I have spent much of my adult life working in the food and retail industry, I have since been inspired to pursue a career in programming. Through my studies at Thinkful, I hope to realize my dream to become a part of a team of passionate developers and create meaningful works that may one day make the lives of others easier.

Part of what I hope to accomplish by pursuing Web Development is to teach and inspire others. Throughout my studies there were many teachers and professors that took a huge role in shaping who I am today. From the way they taught, to the life lessons they shared, I very much wish to return this love for teaching every chance I get. In my time at Starbucks I have trained over thirty five partners, and by growing a small community on twitch.tv, I have a chance to share my knowledge and showcase my skills in things I am very passionate about. I want to give back to the community that has helped me countless times over the years.

About Me

A large part of my decision and drive to become a Web Developer comes from the realization that I truly enjoy the methodical thought process of conceiving a solution to problems others might have. I know that over time, as my knowledge of languages grow, I will have more tools at my disposal to translate desire into functionality. Ultimately, I hope that one day I may learn from and work with a team of like-minded programmers to create a platform that can be a safe place to teach and guide others in their journey of self improvement as well. Life is rough for so many of us, and any chance that I may have to lift someone from that darkness is not one I would ever pass up.

As I mentioned previously, one of my biggest passions is playing video games. I have many memories of Saturday afternoons with my parents and younger brother playing World of Warcraft together. Whenever my dad would buy a new game, I would spend all day sitting next to him just watching him take on the biggest bosses and craziest puzzles. In this, I found a love for playing games and streaming. For years I wanted to start a stream of my own on twitch.tv so that I could entertain others in the same way I was grossly entertained growing up. Combining this with my passion for teaching, I have found a niche of informative, yet exciting gameplay in hopes of recreating that moment of awe with others.

As my career in Web Development is getting started, I can currently utilize the following languages:

  • JavaScript
  • HTML
  • CSS
